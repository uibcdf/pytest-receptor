# Resolved Proposals

Proposals that have been acted on, kept with their resolution attached.

Moved here from [`../pending_proposals/`](../pending_proposals/README.md) once a
proposal has been accepted and implemented, so the inbox stays an inbox. This is
the counterpart to [`../resolved_bugs/`](../resolved_bugs/README.md) for input
that asked the plugin to do something differently rather than reporting it doing
something wrong.

The other two triage outcomes keep their own homes: a proposal *rejected* with a
reason goes to [`../superseded_proposals.md`](../superseded_proposals.md), and one
*accepted as a documented limitation* stays wherever the limitation is recorded.
Nothing is closed by deletion.

## Why these are kept

- They are the **evidence** for their register entries. Deleting them would leave
  the decision in the work queue with nothing behind it.
- They record **the reasoning at the time**, including the options rejected,
  which is what stops a settled question being reopened from scratch.
